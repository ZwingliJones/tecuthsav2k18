R & D in Internet of Things (IoT) using NetSim

By TETCOS, Bangalore

Why Internet of Things? The Internet of Things (IoT) is predicted to be the next big thing in technology and will be the most important factor impacting fundamental business logic in the coming decades. It is defined as the network of physical objects - devices, vehicles, buildings and other items - embedded with sensors, electronics, software and network connectivity that enables these objects to get the ability to sense and communicate.

    Internet of Things (IoT) in NetSim:
  • Modelling and simulation of IOT Networks using NetSim
  • Advanced analytics for IoT network performance
  • Simulation RPL protocol for LLNs
  • NetSim COM Automation Client - MATLAB Interfacing
  • Optimizing Energy Consumption
  • Securing IoT - Modelling Attacks and countermeasures
  • Interfacing with IoT devices (Raspberry Pi, Sensor motes etc.,)
